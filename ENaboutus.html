About Us

SINCE NKOMUN’S ESTABLISHMENT, ITS BIGGEST AIM IS TO CREATE A COMMUNITY THAT CONNECTS STUDENTS AT HIGH SCHOOL FOR THE GIFTED - VIETNAM NATIONAL UNIVERSITY WITH SIMILAR INTERESTS IN MODEL UN TO LEARN AND IMPROVE THEMSELVES THROUGH ACCOMPANYING EACH OTHER IN EVENTS AND ACTIVITIES OF NKOMUN. WHAT’S FURTHER FROM THIS END IS OUR DESIRE TO ESTABLISH A STRONG FOUNDATION SO THAT OUR MEMBERS ARE WELL-PREPARED TO TACKLE ALL THE CHALLENGES IN OTHER MUN EVENTS.

Purpose

With the spirit of spreading this extracurricular activity in mind, NKOMUN will organize numerous workshops assisting students to practice parts that are considered pretty difficult for newcomers in order to get them well-prepared for the main Conference. We will also host Mock MUNs - small-scale conferences so that attendees can gain formal experiences as well as find like-minded individuals who could be their “partners in crime”. Moreover, an official NKOMUN Conference every summer is our biggest ambition after all!
