NKOMUN

FOUNDED IN SEPTEMBER 2021, NANG KHIEU OPEN MODEL UNITED NATIONS - NKOMUN IS A STUDENT-RUN ORGANIZATION LOCATED IN HCMC TO BRING THE CONCEPT OF MUN CONFERENCES CLOSER TO YOUNGSTERS AS WHOLEHEARTEDLY AS POSSIBLE.

MODEL UNITED NATIONS



Model United Nations is an internationally popular academic activity that helps encourage students to know more about connections between countries, the foreign office, and how the United Nations work.

WHY NKOMUN WAS FOUNDED



Nang Khieu Open Model United Nations will take part in creating a MUN community in high school environments and develop the Model United Nations that has been well-known lately.
